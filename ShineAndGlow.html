SHINE & GLOWN

PROBLEM

After looking at other websites, I noticed that hair salons usually do not try to establish a close relationship with customers; rather, they just show basic information like prices and promotions

SOLUTION

For this specific project, I started with the information architecture, trying to consider all possible scenarios that clients could deal with. One of the features I added was the “BOOK ONLINE” functionality. Also, I incorporated a capacity so that customers have the opportunity to see haircut options, news related to fashion and an online store.
